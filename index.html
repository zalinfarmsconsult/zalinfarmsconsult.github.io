Health Benefits of Catfish

Catfish is more than a business – it’s a healthy protein source. Rich in omega-3 fatty acids, lean protein, and essential vitamins like B12 and D, catfish supports heart health, brain function, and muscle growth. It’s low in saturated fat and easy to digest, making it ideal for children, athletes, and health-conscious consumers. Unlike red meat, catfish has a small environmental footprint and provides affordable nutrition. We educate our clients and customers on the nutritional value of catfish to boost demand and promote healthier diets in our communities. Eating farmed catfish supports both your health and local farmers.
